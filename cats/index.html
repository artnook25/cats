Representations of the Cat

Cats are often viewed as creatures of mystery, comfort, and naps. Artists have long been interested in capturing the essence of their subjects, which are as wide-ranging as are things in the universe. The following artists have each interpreted their views of the cat for us to see.

  • American, unknown
  • Eugène Delacroix
  • Betty Fuerst
  • Gwen John
  • Eadweard Muybridge
  • Theophile Alexandre Steinlen
